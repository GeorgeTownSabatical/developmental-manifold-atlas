# Modeling Principles

Organisms are trajectories through developmental state-space. Measurements are coordinates. Fertility and morphology are emergent patterns of that geometry.

## Required Corrections

- body size
- sex
- age
- developmental stage
- measurement quality
- method and instrument
- species or breed
- phylogeny where relevant

## Model Families

- descriptive morphometrics
- allometric scaling
- residual analysis
- multivariate regression
- mixed-effects models
- phylogenetic correction
- Bayesian hierarchical models
- causal diagrams
- manifold learning
- topological data analysis
- sheaf-theoretic integration
