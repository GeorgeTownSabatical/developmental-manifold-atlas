# Outreach Message

Subject: Open developmental morphology and fertility atlas - request for feedback

I am helping launch an open-source research infrastructure project called Developmental Manifold Atlas. The goal is to create shared schemas, measurement standards, dashboards, and hypothesis-testing tools for studying reproductive anatomy, craniofacial geometry, neonatal development, fertility outcomes, genetics, and environmental exposure as coupled developmental trajectories.

The project is early-stage and explicitly ethics-first. We are not seeking individual sensitive human data in the public repository. The initial goal is to build open schemas, aggregate-data standards, reproducible analysis tools, and a cross-disciplinary hypothesis registry.

I would be grateful for feedback on measurement standards, data governance, and whether your field has datasets or protocols that should be represented.
