# Fertility Measurement Protocol v0.1

## Male Measures

- semen concentration
- total motile count
- morphology
- testicular volume
- reproductive hormone panel

## Female Measures

- AMH
- antral follicle count
- FSH
- LH
- ovarian or uterine morphology
- puberty timing

## Assisted Reproduction Measures

- IVF or IUI cycle metadata
- embryo quality summary
- implantation outcome
- pregnancy outcome
- live birth outcome

Public records must be aggregate, synthetic, or metadata-only unless explicitly approved for open release.
